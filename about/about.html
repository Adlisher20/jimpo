JIMPO

Our story

Jimpo – translates to ‘taste’ in Ladakhi language offers a range of Sea Buckthorn based products, located in Ladakh, only place in India where the plant grows.

Sea Buckthorn is a very less explored ‘magical plant’, in fact the only plant found in the face of earth which contains all types of omegas (Omega - 3, 6 and 9 and rare Omega 7) which helps in body building and maintain a healthy body. They're key to the structure of every cell wall we have. They're also an energy source and help keep your heart, lungs, blood vessels and immune system working the way they should. Even though sea buckthorn comes with such huge medicinal benefits, due to rarity and unawareness, potential of the plant is not being utilized.

The story of Jimpo began with the idea of filling this gap and provide the consumers with the exclusivity of sea buckthorn. The company was established in 2018 under the parent company Singhey Enterprises in Phyang Leh, Ladakh now offers a wide range of products including Jams, Juices, Squash and Fruit Pulp.

It all started when Thinless Singhey Rinchan, then a student in The Central University of Jammu recognized the potential nutritious and health benefits of the Sea Buckthorn berry is something which should be recognized. The spark came when, during his holidays from university, met with Anas, a traveler from Kerala, A southwestern state of India. During a long conversation, Thinless had told Anas about the Berries. It was the first time he heard about such a plant. As a practicing Doctor, Anas was surprised about the medicinal and nutritious value of the the plant. It was then Thinless realized that the gem has been hidden for a long time. The idea of Jimpo was conceived and Thinless Enterprises was born.

Today Jimpo produces around 50,000 bottles of various products every month. Each berry is hand harvested from mountains over 12,000 ft, containerized with in hours of harvest and produced from the manufacturing plant situated in Leh. This quick processing contains the quality, nutrients and all the goodness Sea Buckthorn has to offer.

Jimpo is made with the best Sea Buckthorn available. Since harvesting can affect quality of the product, at most care is given to the berries while harvesting. Jimpo supports the local villagers who have been harvesting the product for a very long time. The health benefit of sea Buckthorn has been known to these villagers for. A very long time. In fact the medicinal value of Sea Buckthorn was also recoded in ‘Rgud bzi’, a Tibetan medicinal classic dated back to 8th century AD and is the only plant found in the face of earth which contains all the Omega fatty acids.
